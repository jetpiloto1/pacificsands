Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I schedule a site visit?

▼

Contact us through the form above or call us directly. We'll arrange a convenient time for your visit and provide directions to the development. Site visits typically take 1-2 hours and include a tour of available lots and the surrounding area.

What financing options are available?

▼

We work with several Mexican banks that offer mortgages to foreign buyers. We also offer installment plans for qualified buyers. Contact us to discuss your specific financing needs and we'll connect you with the appropriate financial institutions.

How long does the purchase process take?

▼

The typical purchase process takes 60-90 days from offer to closing. This includes due diligence, fideicomiso setup, and notary closing. We'll guide you through each step and keep you informed of progress.

Can I build immediately after purchase?

▼

Yes, you can begin construction planning immediately after closing. We can provide recommendations for architects, builders, and contractors familiar with the area. Building permits typically take 30-60 days to obtain.

What are the ongoing costs of ownership?

▼

Ongoing costs include annual fideicomiso fees ($300-800), property taxes (0.1-0.3% of assessed value), and any homeowners association fees. We'll provide a detailed breakdown of all costs during your consultation.
